What is Autocopy?
NAGA Autocopy is an innovative feature that lets you automatically copy leading traders on the platform and gain from their experience. Autocopy enables you to trade like a expert. Find the traders you want to copy from the leaderboard and simply hit Autocopy.
How does autocopy trading work?
It’s pretty simple. Choose the trader you’d like to Autocopy, input the amount of funds you’d like to allocate and click Autocopy. You just started mirroring the leading trader’s positions automatically and in real time! You can close and individual trades or pause autocopying any trader, any time you want.
